About Apple Salon

Apple Salon is a modern unisex salon located in the heart of Vaishali Nagar, Jaipur, offering professional grooming, beauty, and styling services for men and women. The salon combines contemporary trends with personalised care to deliver a premium experience.

Our trained professionals use high-quality products and the latest techniques to ensure every client leaves feeling confident, refreshed, and stylish. Hygiene, comfort, and customer satisfaction remain our top priorities.

Whether it is a regular haircut or a special occasion makeover, Apple Salon is your trusted destination for complete grooming solutions.
